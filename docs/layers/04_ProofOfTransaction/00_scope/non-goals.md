# Non-goals — PoT

- Computing fee, commission split, or mint amounts  
- Appraising assets (valuation is institutional input only)  
- Staking, deposits, or slash of held capital  
- Eye executive veto or rollback of a recorded verdict  
- Replacing NodeChain as SoT  
- Per-asset-class subset of P1–P4 in v1 (all four always apply)  
- Portal / UI
